Eggwraps

15 min. 183 kcal 1 serving

Eggwrap is stunningly easy to make and it's also an ingenious meal for you who eat low carb or ketogenic. Egg wraps can be filled with delicious toppings of the variety you prefer or you can fill it with vegetables, meat or fish - or a combinations of those if you prefer. Use these as a replacement for regular tortillas and wraps. Fill, fold and roll!

Diary-free
Gluten-free
Lowcarb
Egg

Ingredients

  • 1 whole egg
  • 2 egg white
  • salt & pepper
  • chives or other herbs

Instructions

  1. Whisk or mash together eggs, salt and pepper with a hand mixer.
  2. Melt a little butter or oil in a frying pan and pour over the egg mixture, sprinkle finely chopped chives or herps as desired on one side before the egg yolk hardens. Turn and fry a little on the other side.
  3. Fill with whatever you want. As good lukewarm as cooled.
